Welcome!

This website was created to support youth who are transitioning out of shelters, group homes, or care systems into safe, stable, and independent living. The Follow-Up Supports Program at Covenant House Toronto extends care beyond shelter walls, providing ongoing guidance to help young people maintain housing, build life skills, and connect with education, employment, and community resources. Our trauma-informed and strengths-based approach ensures that every youth is met with dignity, compassion, and respect. Through practical supports like budgeting, cooking, time management, and advocacy with landlords or service providers, we help youth gain confidence and independence. By gathering verified public resources and mapping essential programs across Ontario, this site serves as a bridge to stability, empowerment, and long-term success.
